#Introduction / Key Points of LeSS Videos

LeSS of a Story: An Introduction to Large-Scale Scrum (LeSS) - Agile Tour Singapore Nov 2015 This video showcases one of the creators of the LeSS management framework (Bas Vodde) introducing the LeSS management framework during the Agile Tour Singapore convention in November 2015. Bas Vodde gives the history of how the LeSS management framework came to be, why it works, and how it works.

Webinar summary: Seven Agile adoption problems with LeSS solutions by Rowan Bunning This video showcases the LeSS management framework in the span of roughly 45 minutes and why it’s effective at managing software development by tackling tough issues faced by other software management styles today.

Short Introduction to LeSS Don’t have a lot of time on your hand? This video introduces the LeSS management framework in 8 minutes by Craig Larman (who is one of the creators of the LeSS management framework).

LeSS vis a vis SAFE In this video the LeSS management framework is being compared to another management framework SAFe. This video highlights the pros and cons of both management frameworks.

Meetup: Large-Scale Scrum (LESS) by Craig Larman This video showcases Craig Larman (one of the creators of the LeSS management framework) talking and describing LeSS at the NDC software conference in 2015.

Systems Optimization & Organizational Design: a LeSS Perspective This video showcases Craig Larman (one of the creators of the LeSS management framework) talking and describing LeSS with a large product group.

LeSS of a Story: Introduction to LeSS at Xebicon 2015 This video showcases Bas Vodde (one of the creators of the LeSS management framework) talking and describing how LeSS came to fruition based on the experiences of software management he (Bas Vodde) and Craig Larman have had in the past.

LeSS in organisations by Ran Nyman This video showcases Ran Nyman (a Certified LeSS trainer from Gosei Ltd.) talking about the LeSS framework at Agile Warsaw Meetup. The talk is a short introduction to some basic ideas behind the LeSS framework. Interviews with respect to LeSS
